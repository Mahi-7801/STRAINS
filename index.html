Background

Rooted in the Garment Belt

Born and raised in the heart of Tamil Nadu's textile corridor, Naga Praharsha Govindu grew up witnessing the raw power of garment manufacturing at scale. Tirupur — India's knitwear capital — wasn't just a backdrop; it was a living classroom where the economics of fabric, labor, and global supply chains unfolded daily.

After years of hands-on experience across factory operations, production planning, and merchant export logistics, Praharsha identified a critical gap: manufacturing ran on intuition while digital marketing ran on data — and the two never spoke. This realization became the founding thesis of Strains Group.

How the Ecosystem Flows

Raw organic materials enter through Stitchery where they are processed, cut, and stitched into finished garments. These feed directly into Fashions for design curation and D2C distribution. Meanwhile, Associates produces cinematic catalog content and manages performance ad campaigns that drive demand. Sales data flows back to optimize Stitchery's production scheduling, creating a true closed-loop system. The Incubator serves as the entry point for new brands to access the entire pipeline.

Raw Fabrics Loop

Strains Stitchery outputs customized organic threads feeding directly into Fashions' product creation, bypassing middle agents entirely. Real-time inventory data flows back to guide reorder scheduling.

D2C Design Pipeline

Strains Fashions drives premium label aesthetics — plant-based weaves, vegan silk, and zero-waste botanical seed packaging. Every design is engineered for both runway appeal and manufacturing efficiency.

Cinematic Production

In-house HD advertising, catalog shoots, editorial reels, and scriptwriting that position brands at luxury streetwear benchmarks. Full post-production with color grading and sound design included.

Creative Media Loop

Associates synchronizes cinematic output with Fashions' designer mockups ensuring brand identity is held across all media channels. Campaign assets are A/B tested before full rollout.

Conversion Analytics

Performance marketing data from Ads guides Stitchery's manufacturing capacities based on live conversion demand signals. Inventory is allocated dynamically across channels based on real-time ROAS metrics.

Brand Incubator Lab

Our incubator maps new brands from fabric selection to operational scale — bridging Stitchery capacity and Fashions design into a launch roadmap. Includes go-to-market strategy and performance forecasting.

Our facility spans 12,000 sq. ft. with dedicated cutting, stitching, finishing, and quality control zones. Equipped with 60+ industrial machines including Juki lockstitch, overlock, and flatlock units capable of handling complex garment constructions from sampling through bulk production.

Strains Fashions

Strains Fashions sets the aesthetic benchmark for premium organic streetwear. We utilize raw plant-based weaves, vegan silk, and plantable labels. When detached from your garment, simply cover the price tag in a pot with soil and watch it grow into fresh botanical herbs.

Each collection follows a zero-waste design philosophy — pattern layouts are optimized by proprietary nesting software to minimize fabric offcuts, and remnant materials are repurposed into accessories, tote bags, and limited-edition patchwork pieces. Our signature dye process uses turmeric, indigo, pomegranate peel, and madder root to achieve rich natural color profiles without chemical mordants.
